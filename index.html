A BIT ABOUT ME:



I'm a drummer for more than two thirds of my life,

while working as a barista in Meir hospital.

But as soon as I got the opportunity, me and my friend bought a recording studio.
Our primery work was recording and filming songs for spacial occations
like birthdays, weddings, bar and bat mitzvas and much more.
I've had the studio for more than 11 years, until the start of 2024.
Since my studio closed, I searched for a new career path,
And as soon as I learned about QA I thought I would be perfect for it.
